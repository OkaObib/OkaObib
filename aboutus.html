ABOUT NUGGY

OUR HISTORY AND PRODUCTS

Nuggy started in Nov, 10 2022. As of the moment we are one of the popular fast food brands in the Philippines. With a strict adherence to the highest standards of food quality, service and cleanliness, Nuggy serves great-tasting, high-quality and affordable food products. Each meals that you order comes along with a free french fries for you to enjoy, and if you're vegan we also provide you some plant-based chicken nuggets that is very healthy and nutritiuos.
We also have many flavors of dipping sauces to choose from such as, Mayonnaise, Tomato Ketchup, Cheese, and Mustard.
